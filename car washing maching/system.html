auto moving nose

Located on both sides of the train track, these separate but parallel Nose washing units with phase difference move on the track. With the automatic moving nose washing unit, especially the nose sections of High Speed Trains with special profile are washed. The vehicle is stopped and the nose is washed. Thanks to the special moving design of the brush system, the entire vehicle nose is washed perfectly. The units have chemical dosing and spraying systems on them. The system has the ability to move robotically forward and backward along the nose of the vehicle on special rails.
